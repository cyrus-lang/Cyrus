# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to maintaining a strictly professional environment. All participants, including contributors, maintainers, and users, are required to adhere to the following standards:

## Expected Behavior

- Conduct must be professional, objective, and focused solely on the project and its goals.
- Communication should be clear, concise, and relevant; avoid unnecessary commentary or distractions.
- Respect for all participants is mandatory. Personal differences or opinions must not interfere with collaboration.
- All contributions and interactions must comply with applicable laws and regulations.

## Prohibited Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or any form of abusive behavior will not be tolerated.
- Personal attacks, insults, inflammatory remarks, or language that could be construed as hostile are forbidden.
- Posting content that is offensive, derogatory, or irrelevant to the project will result in immediate action.
- Attempts to undermine or disrupt the project’s workflow, including spamming or repeated non-constructive behavior, are prohibited.

## Enforcement

- Violations of this Code of Conduct will be taken seriously.
- Maintainers reserve the right to remove any contributions, block access, or ban individuals who do not comply.
- Reports of misconduct should be directed to the maintainers through the designated communication channels.
- All reports will be reviewed impartially, and appropriate action will be taken based on the severity of the offense.

## Commitment

By participating in this project, you acknowledge and accept these conditions. Failure to comply may result in restricted access or removal from the project without prior notice.

This Code of Conduct is non-negotiable and enforced uniformly to maintain the integrity and professionalism of the project.
